Pick a Card

I've been dabbling with closeup magic for years! In college, I decided to take it seriously enough to perform at several events. Whether it's a corporate event, a wedding, or a restaurant, live magic is never going away as few other visual spectacles can inspire the same type of wonder.

I'd love to perform a strolling show for your next big event!

Pause for Effect

While live magic is here to stay, the landscape of magic has changed. The future is becoming increasingly digital. With that guiding principle, I co-host Pause for Effect, a magic channel engaging in reviews, tutorial, and lifestyle content.

I invite you to take a look whether you're already a magician or just looking to get into magic.
