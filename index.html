About Me

Growing up, I've always been a science guy.

I went to school for Neuroscience and my favorite part of all my classes was setting up experiments, taking measurements, crunching the data, and revising the set up.

So when I started applying for jobs every interviewer always asked me why I would pursue a career in analytics.

Honestly, at the end of the day the core concepts of measuring and evaluating key indicators are the same. The only difference is in what's being added up.

Whether you're a small business owner looking to dip your feet into analytics or a small agency looking to add some analytics expertise to your offerings, let's connect about how a resource with 4 years of analytics experience working with enterprise clients can assist you today!

Services

Analytics Audits

I'll take a look at your site and identify key areas that could yield you significant analytics insights. Google Analytics access preferred but not required.

Setup

I'll set up tags for you in Google Tag Manager and create a Google Analtyics instance if you don't already have one. Otherwise, I'll ensure that your Google Analytics instance is up to best practice standards for GA standard and GA4.

Google Analytics Investigations

Do you see any issues with your GA data? I'll take a look and hunt down the causes of any discrepancies for you! Access to sources will be required.

Analytics Documentation

Going hand in hand with strategy, I'll make a detailed spreadsheet to serve as your data dicitonary. If you have developer resources, I'll also create implementation specs for them regarding more complicated dataLayer implementations.

Analytics Strategy

Let's sit and have a candid talk about an analytics road map for your business and what events make sense to track when. Then let's take it a step further and talk about what to do with that data.

Analytics Insights

Do you already have data? Let's take a look at it and see where you can make improvements to your business! I can spin up a Data Studio report to track key performance indicators so you will always have a empirical pulse on your business.
