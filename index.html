About Me

and my interests

I started my analytics career at an analytics agency in 2017. I reasoned that the techniques I honed to interpret data in Neuroscience could be used to interpret business data as well.

The friends I made at the start of my career helped me realize how much I enjoy programming from querying tables in BigQuery to writing python scripts and solving coding challenges. Thanks to them, I am delving into my journey to learn as much as I can about programming!

Outside of programming and analytics, I have wide range of interests including gaming and magic. I've partnered with a fellow magician to create a Youtube channel to share thoughts on and showcase the close up magic I've practiced for seven years.

Last but not least, I am also an investor focusing on long term equities trading based on company fundamentals and macro-economic trends.
